Cars are automobiles that can transport people.
It is the main means of travelling for hundreds
of millions of people all over the world. Cars
have changed the way we live probably more
than any other invention in history. At first
only a few people had cars but after a while more
and more people bought them because they improved
the way people lived. Farmers with cars were able
to bring their products to places that were
farther away. The appearance of cities and towns
also changed. More and more workers drove to their
jobs and people started to move to suburbs outside
the town centers.Automobiles give people many jobs.
Millions of people around the world work in
factories where cars are produced. Millions more
work at gas stations, restaurants or motels that
travelers stop at.However, cars also cause problems.
Millions of people die in car accidents every year.
Automobiles pollute the air that we breathe and
parking space in cities is scarce because everyone
wants to use their cars to get to city centers.
